Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about charges, verification, and our operational models.

How are logistics charges calculated? ▼

Our charges are computed dynamically based on distance, vehicle type requirement, total helpers/manpower needed, packaging materials selected, and the nature of the cargo items.

Are vendors verified before onboarding? ▼

Yes. Every driver, loader, and packer agency undergoes strict background verification, operational experience reviews, and service quality checks before joining the Ampy Logi network.

Do you provide both B2B and B2C services? ▼

Yes. Ampy Logi operates B2B services (office shifting, commercial logistics) as well as B2C services (household shifting, vehicle relocation) under the same unified system.

Can I request a customized quotation? ▼

Yes, absolutely. We evaluate special transport projects, heavy freight, and complex industrial relocations individually to supply personalized cost structures.
